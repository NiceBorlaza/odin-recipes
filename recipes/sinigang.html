Pork Sinigang Recipe

Description

Sinigang is a sour soup native to the Philippines. This recipe uses pork as the main ingredient

Ingredients:

  • 1 pc onion, sliced
  • 1 pc peeled and sliced ginger, pounded lightly
  • 3 tbsp MAGNOLIA NUTRI OIL PALM VEGETABLE OIL
  • 1/2 kg MONTEREY PORK SINIGANG CUT OR ADOBO CUT
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 pc chicken bouillon cube
  • 1 stalk lemon grass (tanglad), tied into a knot
  • 4 pc sinigang mix
  • 4 pc siling haba
  • 1/2 cup tomato ketchup
  • 1 bundle pechay (bok choy)
  • 1 tsp iodized fine salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Procedure:

  1. In a large stockpot, sauté onion and ginger in oil over medium heat. Add pork and sauté until meat changes color.
  2. Add water, chicken cube and tanglad. Cover and simmer for about 30 minutes or until meat is fork tender.
  3. Add sinigang mix, sili, ketchup, pechay, salt

Pork Adobo

Description

Adobo is a kind of cooking method, originating from the Philippines. It involves simmering meat and even seafood in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, and garlic.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kilo pork belly
  • 2 tablespoons garlic, minced or crushed
  • 5 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 4 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon peppercorn
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon Magic Sarap or MSG
  • 1 tablespoon sugar to taste
  • salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Combine pork belly, soy sauce and garlic, Marinate for at least one hour
  2. Heat the pot and put-in marinated pork, Panfry until brown
  3. Pour remaining marinade including garlic.
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, dried bay leaves, sugar and Magic Sarap or MSG. Bring to boil, Simmer for 40 minutes to one hour.
  5. Put-in vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes
  6. Add salt to taste.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
